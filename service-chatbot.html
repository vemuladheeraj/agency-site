Chatbot FAQs

Do I need to buy a separate phone number for the WhatsApp chatbot? +

Yes. To use the official WhatsApp Cloud API, you need a clean phone number that does not have an active WhatsApp app account. We will set this up on your number of choice.

Are there extra costs from Meta for messaging? +

Meta provides 1,000 free service conversations per month. Beyond that, Meta charges a very small fee per conversation (approx. 30 paise for utility and 80 paise for marketing). We will help you link your credit card directly to Meta Developer dashboard.

Can the chatbot handle regional Indian languages? +

Yes. Because we use advanced LLMs (like Claude and GPT models), the chatbot can interpret and reply in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hinglish, and 12+ other regional languages seamlessly.

What if the AI makes a mistake or answers wrong? +

We restrict the AI using strict "system prompts" and "grounding documents" so it only answers using your official FAQ lists. If it cannot answer a question, it automatically triggers a live agent handover alert.

Does this link with Google Calendar? +

Absolutely. We link the chatbot directly with your Google Calendar, Calendly, or custom booking database via Make/Zapier, ensuring bookings are updated instantly with no overlaps.
