Pricing & Policy FAQs

Do you take advance payment? +

Yes. We typically begin with an initial deposit to start planning and design work, then complete the remaining balance upon delivery and approval.

How long does a typical project take? +

Standard marketing websites usually take a few business days to a couple of weeks depending on scope, while larger custom builds may take longer.

What if I need changes after the website is delivered? +

We include post-launch support for minor updates and fixes, and we can also discuss ongoing maintenance support if you need continued help after launch.

Do you provide ongoing monthly support after launch? +

Yes. We can offer ongoing maintenance plans depending on how much support, updates, or troubleshooting your site needs each month.

Can I pay in installments? +

For larger custom projects, we can discuss a phased payment structure that fits the delivery timeline and your comfort level.
