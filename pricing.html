Pricing & Policy FAQs

Do you take advance payment? +

Yes. For Starter and Growth plans, we work on a 50/50 model: 50% upfront to initiate process mapping and draft designs, and 50% upon delivery and staging approval, right before domain release.

How long does a typical project take? +

Standard marketing websites (Starter/Growth) take 5-10 business days. Custom dashboards or automated lead pipelines (Pro) take 2-3 weeks depending on the complexity of database logic.

What if I need changes after the website is delivered? +

All plans include 30 days of free post-launch support for text corrections, image updates, or minor bug resolutions. Beyond 30 days, you can choose our hourly updates or low-cost monthly care plans.

Do you provide ongoing monthly support after launch? +

Yes. We offer Webmaster Maintenance plans starting at ₹3,000/month, which includes monthly plugin updates, site backups, speed tuning checks, and 2 hours of direct design changes.

Can I pay in installments? +

For custom pro projects exceeding ₹50,000, we offer a 3-part payment structure: 40% advance to start, 30% midpoint on design lock-in, and 30% on launch. We accept UPI, Net Banking, and Credit Cards.
