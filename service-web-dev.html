Web Dev FAQs

Do you build on WordPress, Framer, or custom code? +

We choose what fits your business best. For marketing websites, we prefer Framer or static HTML/CSS because they are lightning fast and secure. For custom booking portals or dashboards, we write React or static JS solutions.

Who purchases the domain and hosting? +

You buy the domain on Hostinger or GoDaddy under your account so you maintain 100% legal ownership. We will guide you on the best plan, configure SSL records, and deploy the codes for you.

Will I be able to edit the website content myself later? +

Yes. If built on Framer, you get a clean editor link to change text/images in 1 click. If built on custom HTML, we comment the code clearly and provide a 5-minute training video on how to change lines.

Do you provide SEO optimization? +

Yes. Every website we build includes meta tags setup, page load optimizations, Google Search Console indexing request, and sitemap configuration to make sure you show up on Google search lists.

What is your payment model? +

We work on a milestone model: 50% advance payment upfront, and 50% upon delivery and staging approval, right before connecting the files to your active domain.
